    You can easily move in the city using Lyon’s wide range of public transport. It’s also a beautiful city for a walk, if you fancy.
  </p>

<section class="container content spacer-t-b">
  <div class="boxes boxes-4">
      <div class="box">
        <h3>Metro, Tram, Buses</h3>
        <img src="/img/photos/lyon-scene7-Tram.jpg" alt="River confluence" style="width:100%;">
        <p>You can move around Lyon with 5 efficient <a href="http://www.tcl.fr/en/Getting-Around/Getting-around" target="_blank">metro lines</a>, 5 tram lines and 138 bus lines.</p>
        <p>The average ticket price is 1,80&euro;, Get 10 in a bundle for a little more than 16&euro;.  The weekly pass is around 15&euro;.</p>
      </div>
      <div class="box">
        <h3>Rental bikes</h3>
        <img src="/img/photos/lyon-scene1-Bikes.jpg" alt="River confluence" style="width:100%;">
        <p>The <a href="https://velov.grandlyon.com/en.html" target="_blank">V&eacute;lo&rsquo;v</a> rental bike system is easy access and pretty cheap. Pick any bike for 1 &euro; per day, then you can ride 30 minutes for free every time you pick a bike. </p>
        <p>More than 300 stations are available today and you can pick and leave your bike wherever you want.</p>
      </div>
      <div class="box">
        <h3>Taxis</h3>
        <img src="/img/photos/lyon-scene9-Taxi.jpg" alt="River confluence" style="width:100%;">
        <p>You can use Lyon&rsquo;s extensive <a href="https://www.taxilyonnais.com/taxi-lyon/EN-Estimation.html" target="_blank">taxi</a> network to go almost anywhere for under 20&euro;.</p>
        <p>Trip from the airport to the city center will be &plusmn;50&euro;.</p>
      </div>
      <div class="box">
        <h3>Walking</h3>
        <img src="/img/photos/lyon-scene5-Vieux-Lyon.jpg" alt="River confluence" style="width:100%;">
        <p>Lyon is a very walkable city. You can wander around the center and reach easily most spots.</p>
      </div>
      <div class="box">
        <h3>Boats</h3>
        <img src="/img/photos/lyon-scene8-Vaporetto.jpg" alt="River confluence" style="width:100%;">
        <p>There is a boat service from Presqu&rsquo;&icirc;le to the confluence area.</p>
      </div>
  </div>
</section>
