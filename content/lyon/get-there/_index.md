Lyon benefits from a central location in Europe. You can enjoy a quiet high-speed train, or take a direct flight from **125 cities** in **46 countries**. Paris is just two hours away. Bon voyage!

![Flights to Lyon](/img/graphics/lyon_flights.png)

<div class="boxes boxes-4">
<div class="box">
<h2>By plane</h2>
<img src="/img/photos/lyon-scene10-Airport.jpg" alt="Lyon Saint Exupery airport (LYS)" style="width:100%;">
<p>You&rsquo;ll arrive at Lyon Saint-Exup&eacute;ry (LYS), the main airport.  From there, there&rsquo;s a 30 minutes <a href="https://www.rhonexpress.fr/en" target="_blank">Rh&ocirc;ne&nbsp;Express</a> that will take you to Gare de Lyon-Part-Dieu.</p>
<p>Lyon is also connected directly to Paris-Charles de Gaulle (CDG) and Geneva Airport (GVA) via a 2 hours direct train ride.</p>
</div>
<div class="box">
<h2>By train</h2>
<img src="/img/photos/lyon-scene11-Train-TGV.jpg" alt="Inside the highspeed train - TGV" style="width:100%;">
<p>You can reach Lyon from many European cities. It takes about two hours from <a href="https://www.trainline.eu/trains/paris/lyon" target="_blank">Paris</a> or <a href="https://www.trainline.eu/trains/geneve-cornavin/lyon" target="_blank">Geneva</a>, five hours from <a href="http://www.eurostar.com/uk-en/city-breaks/france/lyon" target="_blank">London</a>, <a href="https://www.trainline.eu/trains/barcelona/lyon 	" target="_blank">Barcelona</a> or <a href="https://www.trainline.eu/trains/milano/lyon" target="_blank">Milan</a>.</p>
<p>The <strong>Part-Dieu</strong> and <strong>Perrache</strong> stations are both well connected to the metro network.</p>
</div>
<div class="box">
<h2>By car</h2>
<img src="/img/photos/lyon-scene12-Carpooling.jpg" alt="Carpooling" style="width:100%;">
<p>The centrality of Lyon in Europe makes easy to reach it by car, and sharing with others might make an enjoyable road trip too.</p>
<p>For convenient carpooling you can check <a href="https://www.blablacar.co.uk/search?fn=&fc=&tn=Lyon%2C+France" target="_blank">Bla&nbsp;Bla&nbsp;Car</a>.</p>
</div>
</div>

## Visas

If you are a citizen on the list of countries that are [exempt](http://www.diplomatie.gouv.fr/en/coming-to-france/getting-a-visa/article/foreign-nationals-holding-ordinary-passports-exempt-from-visa-requirements) for stays up to 90 days, excellent! Otherwise you might need a VISA.

Make sure you [check](http://www.diplomatie.gouv.fr/en/coming-to-france/getting-a-visa/article/faq-visas) before travelling.
