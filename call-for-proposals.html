    Eager to speak at Interaction 18?

    Dissolving boundaries.
    Building connections.

    Thank you for considering submitting to Interaction 18.

    At Interaction 18 we would like to reflect on the value and potential of interaction design at three different levels.

    Micro: details that matter
    What are our best practices? How can we design the tools and processes of our craft?

    Macro: systemic transformations
    What are the interesting challenges we are facing today? How can we apply design for strategic and systemic change?

    Meso: identity and vision
    What is our role moving forward? How can we evolve design for the long term vision and the organizations of tomorrow?

    Who is your audience?

    We want to set a stage for advanced, more in-depth topics, so while the conference is open to every level, the majority of the audience is going to be senior designers, tech and creative directors, thought leaders, startup founders, teachers, and beyond.

    Why should you submit to Interaction 18?

    You will inspire people, impacting the discipline of interaction design. You will also meet other design leaders from all around the world. More importantly, it will be fun!

    Proposing a Talk

    We are going to do different lengths to accommodate different scopes, approaches and insights.

    From 7 minutes talks to describe one straightforward use-case or concept, to 15 minutes to expand over a single larger idea with examples, to 35 minutes with a very rich, full-fledged and well articulated concept or historical walkthrough.

    Submit your talk

    Proposing a Workshop

    In the workshops, we’d like you to go one step further and consider an already experienced audience and do deep-dives in the topic from the very beginning.

    We’re going to have a mix of short (4h) and long (8h) workshops. You’ll be guiding your class through a deep dive into a specific topic, with a strong mix of hands-on exercises and frontal lectures.

    Submit your workshop

    What do you have to prepare?

    1. Up to 500 words description of your talk or workshop. Be clear and articulate, give an overview while also adding enough details to show your unique perspective on the subject.
    2. Up to 100 words on how do you imagine engaging the audience.
    3. Up to 100 words on why it makes a good fit for Interaction 18 themes.
    4. An illustration representing the talk or the workshop (a picture, a sketch, whatever you think is self-explanatory).
    5. Up to 250 words biography, to let us know more about you.

    Please note that most material provided can be used on the website to promote you and your talk if it gets selected. We will be giving you the opportunity to review it before publication.

    How will we choose?

    Your proposal will be peer reviewed by experts in our community or our workshop selection committee.

    We will review based on quality, speaker’s experience, and how well it aligns with the conference themes and the other presentations.

    Dates

    • September 13th, 2017 at 11:59pm CET (Central European Time): deadline for submitting the proposals for both talks and workshops.
    • October 13th, 2017: we will inform all workshop applicants if they have been selected.
    • November 2nd, 2017: we will inform all talk applicants if they have been selected.

    Diversity and Accessibility

    We want Interaction 18 to truly represent the diversity and the multiple facets of interaction design. Each voice is important.

    If you’d like support, for translation, coaching, any doubt and inquiry, or if we missed something important, please reach out.

    Speaker informations

    • As a talk speaker you get a small compensation to cover part of your travel expense, and a ticket for the conference.
    • As a workshop speaker, you get half of the sold workshop tickets.
    • Please read our Code of Conduct.
