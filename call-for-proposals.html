    Eager to speak at Interaction 18?

    Dissolving boundaries.
    Building connections.

    Technology and design keep redefining the relationship between us and the world, a continuous flow of change that invests us, objects, society and the environment. At Interaction 18 in Lyon, we want to reflect on what interaction design can do to improve our lives moving forward, what role we want to play within the transformations happening around us, and why it’s important.

    Why should you submit to Interaction 18?

    You will inspire people, impacting the discipline of interaction design. You will also meet other design leaders from all around the world. More importantly, it will be fun!

    Proposing a Talk

    [ needs tracks details ] We are going to have different lengths to accommodate different approaches and insights: 7, 15 and 35 minutes.

    Proposing a Workshop

    We’re going to have a mix of short (4 hours) and long (8 hours) workshops. You’ll be guiding your class through a deep dive into a specific topic, with a strong mix of hands-on exercises and frontal lectures.

    What do you have to prepare?


    • Up to 500 words description of your talk or workshop. Be clear and articulate, give an overview while also adding enough details to show your unique perspective on the subject.
    • Up to 100 words on how do you imagine engaging the audience.
    • Up to 100 words on why it makes a good fit for Interaction 18 themes.
    • An illustration representing the talk or the workshop (a picture, a sketch, whatever you think is self-explanatory).
    • A photo of you at 1920 × 1080 pixels.
    • Up to 250 words biography, to let us know more about you.

    Please note that all the material provided will be used on the website to promote your and your talk if it gets selected.

    How do we choose?

    Your proposal will be peer reviewed by experts in our community or our workshop selection committee. We are going to review based on quality, speaker’s experience, and how well it aligns with the conference themes.

    Dates


    • September 15th, 2017 at 11:59pm CET (Central European Time): deadline for submitting the proposals for both talks and workshops.
    • October 15th, 2017: we will inform all workshop applicants if they have been selected.
    • November 1st, 2017: we will inform all talk applicants if they have been selected.

    Diversity and Accessibility

    We want Interaction 18 to truly represent the diversity and the multiple facets of interaction design. Each voice is important. If you need specific support – for example, for translation or coaching – or you have any doubt, you can reach out to us here. Also, if we might have missed something important, please let us know.

    Speaker informations

    • As a talk speaker you get 500€, and a ticket for the conference.
    • As a workshop speaker, you get half of the sold workshop tickets, and a ticket for the conference.
    • Please read our Code of Conduct.
